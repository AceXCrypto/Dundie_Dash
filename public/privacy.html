Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily enter:

  • X (Twitter) username
  • Solana wallet address
  • Game scores

2. How We Use It

Your data is used solely to display leaderboard rankings and track high scores within the Game. We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond Game functionality.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Real names or physical addresses
  • IP addresses (we do not log these)
  • Cookies for tracking (we use only localStorage for local game state)
  • Financial information or private keys

4. Wallet Address Usage

Your Solana wallet address is used exclusively as a unique player identifier. We never initiate transactions, request signatures, access your wallet balance, or interact with your wallet in any way beyond storing the address string.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database hosted by Supabase (cloud infrastructure). Access is limited to the Game's backend service using encrypted credentials.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Leaderboard data (username and score) is publicly visible to all Game users by design.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as the Game operates. Weekly scores are reset every Sunday at midnight CST. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us through our Discord server.

8. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. GDPR / International Users

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via our Discord server. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of the Game constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, reach out via our community Discord server.
